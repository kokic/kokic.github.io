Example. 矩阵表示给出的微分运算 [synthetic-differential-000B]

  • May 9, 2024
  • kokic

$\gdef\spaces#1{~ #1 ~}$

设 $u, v \in R^R$, $n \in \Z$. 记 $A(u, u') = (\begin{smallmatrix} u & u' \\ 0 & u \end{smallmatrix})$. 从矩阵运算中立刻得到

  1. $A(u, u') + A(v, v') \spaces= A(u+v, u'+v')$
  2. $A(u, u') - A(v, v') \spaces= A(u-v, u'-v')$
  3. $A(u, u') \cdot A(v, v') \spaces= A(uv, u'v + uv')$
  4. $A(u, u') \cdot (A(v, v'))^{-1} \spaces= A(uv^{-1}, (u'v - uv')v^{-2})$
  5. $(A(u, u'))^n = A(u^n, nu^{n-1}u')$

对于 $e^{A(u,u')}$, 注意 $A(u, u') = A(u, 0) + A(0, u')$, 这里 $(A(0, u))^2 = 0$. 同样可以计算得到 $e^{A(u,u')} = A(e^u, 0) \cdot A(1, u') = A(e^u, e^u u')$. 对于 $\log A(u,u')$ 我们直接展开即可.

$$ \begin{aligned} \log A(u,u') &\spaces= \sum_{k \ge 1}\frac{(-1)^{k+1}}{k} \cdot (A-1)^k \\ &\spaces= \begin{pmatrix} \sum_{k \ge 1}\frac{(-1)^{k+1}}{k} \cdot (u-1)^k & \sum_{k \ge 0} (-1)^k (u-1)^k \cdot u' \\ 0 & \sum_{k \ge 1}\frac{(-1)^{k+1}}{k} \cdot (u-1)^k \end{pmatrix} \\ &\spaces= \begin{pmatrix} \log u & u'u^{-1} \\ 0 & \log u \end{pmatrix} \\ \end{aligned} $$
