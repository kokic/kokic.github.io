Titu 引理又名 [Sedrakyan 不等式](https://handwiki.org/wiki/Sedrakyan%27s_inequality)、Bergström 不等式、[Engel 形式](https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/Cauchy-Schwarz_Inequality). 在 [](./CN2B.md) 中实行替换 $a_k \mapsto \frac{x_k}{\sqrt\ell_k}$, $b_k \mapsto \sqrt\ell_k$, 随后移项即得

$$
\sum_{k=1}^n\dfrac{x_k^2}{\ell_k} 
\spaces\ge \Big(\sum_{k=1}^nx_k\Big)^2 \Big({\sum_{k=1}^n{\ell_k}}\Big)^{-1} 
$$
